About SilvaTech Institute

Founded in 2005, SilvaTech Institute has grown to become a leading technical training and services provider in the region.

Our Story & Mission

SilvaTech Institute was established with a vision to bridge the skills gap in technical trades while providing exceptional services to the community. Over the years, we've trained thousands of skilled professionals who now contribute to various industries across the country.

Our mission is to empower individuals with practical, hands-on technical skills that lead to meaningful employment and career advancement while delivering high-quality technical services to our clients.

Mr. John Silva

Founder & Chairman

With over 30 years in the construction industry, Mr. Silva established SilvaTech to create opportunities for young technicians.

Dr. Maria Rodriguez

Co-Founder & Academic Director

Former engineering professor who designed the institute's curriculum to balance theory with practical application.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive technical services across multiple disciplines with a commitment to quality, safety, and innovation.

Metal Work

Custom fabrication, welding, structural steel work, metal art, and industrial metal solutions with precision and durability.

Plumbing

Residential, commercial, and industrial plumbing systems, pipe installation, maintenance, and emergency repair services.

Mechanics

Automotive, industrial machinery repair and maintenance, engine diagnostics, and mechanical system optimization.

Civil Works

Construction, structural engineering, road works, building projects, and infrastructure development with engineering excellence.
